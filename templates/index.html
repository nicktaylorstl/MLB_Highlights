CLICK HERE TO GET HIGHLIGHTS

Enter a player's name, the team he played for, and a date.

The page will show you any highlight videos featuring the player from games on that date.

You can enter any part of the player's name.

For example: Cincinnati Reds, July 8, 2023, "Elly De La Cruz" or even just "uz" will return Elly stealing all the bases

If you just type "el" you will get Elly, but also Christian Yelich hitting a double against the Reds on that day.

If it shows your search paramaters but no highlights, there were no highlights for the player that day
